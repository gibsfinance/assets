## Session 3 (2026-03-20)

### Server-Side Image Resize (completed, merged to staging)
- `sharp` for on-the-fly resize: `?w=N&h=N&format=webp` on all `/image/` endpoints
- `image_variant` table: DB-persisted variants, rate-limited creation (5/image/min, 100/min global)
- Daily prune job: deletes variants with < 3 hits in 24h, resets counters
- SVG passthrough when viewBox present; rasterize when not
- LINK-mode images (pumptires, dexscreener) fetched and resized on demand
- `x-resize` header on all responses (original/WxH/transcoded)
- 18 unit tests passing
- Spec: `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-03-20-server-image-resize-design.md`
- Plan: `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-03-20-server-image-resize.md`
